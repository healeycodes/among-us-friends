Among Us League

How are the rankings calculated?

Players have a hidden crew Elo and a hidden impostor Elo. These values increase or decrese depending on the opposing team's average Elo. These two values are then averaged to get a general rating.

Q. Why aren't your teammates taken into account?
A. Because it leads to Elo inflation.

Q. Which matters more: your crew or impostor performance?
A. They matter equally.
